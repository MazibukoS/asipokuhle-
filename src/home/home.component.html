Who we are

Asipokuhle Holdings is an investment holding company founded by a South African entrepreneur, with a focus on long-term investment opportunities. We specialize in forging partnerships with financial entities renowned for successful investments across various industries.

Asipokuhle Holdings provides equity and empowerment financing for brownfield and late-stage greenfield projects in South Africa, primarily focusing on renewable energy and economic infrastructure projects such as energy, water, transport, and ICT within South Africa and select parts of the Southern African Development Community (SADC).
